Project information

For the IoT Essentials course I had to make an automated aquarium. It monitored various data and also automatically fed and automatically topped up the water when it was far below its normal water level.

Automatic Aquarium

The first automated task my aquarium did was automatically top up the water. I did this by measuring the distance from a certain point to my water level. If the water level went below a certain point, the water pump, which was connected to a relay, would turn on until the water was replenished to the correct level and then the pump would turn off again. I also had buttons to turn the pump and aquarium lights on and off.

I also set a time when the fish were fed automatically. I did this by attaching a container with a hole to a stepper motor. The stepper motor then started running at the set hour so that the food fell out of the tray, I had set it so that only a certain amount fell out of the tray. Finally, the aquarium was filmed 24/7 and you could view the footage by surfing to the webcam's IP.
